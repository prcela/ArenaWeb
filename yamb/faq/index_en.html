I have a new mobile phone. How can I transfer my Yamb account and my scores to new device?

Both devices must be with iOS 13 or later.

1. Open Yamb on the old device go to your profile and sign in with your Apple ID.

2. Do the same on the new device and you will be signed in on new and signed out from the old device. That's it.

What does my ⭐️ mean?

Stars represent an ELO rating system. Every player starts with 1000⭐️. The crucial feature of Elo rating system is this: beating a higher-rated opponent gives you more points than beating one with a lower rating. Similarly, when you lose to a player with a lower Elo score, your rating will decrease more than if the opponent had a higher initial rating.

To make progress you must be better than yourself, better then expected. Once you reach Your skill level, you probably will stay there.

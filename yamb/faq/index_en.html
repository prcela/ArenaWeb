I got kicked out of the game, but I have good internet

WiFi isn't always the best solution. It uses radio waves, so the speed of your connection depends on how far away you are from the router and how good the router is. The farther away you are from the router, the weaker the signal will be and the slower your connection will be.

Routers that you get from internet service providers are sometimes not the best quality. Because of them, WiFi repeaters are often used, which are garbage. Avoid that. A quality router that is added to the standard router is a better option.

Routers from internet service providers are sometimes not the best quality. Because of that, people often use WiFi repeaters, which are terrible. Avoid them. A better option is to get a quality router that you can add to your standard router.

However, sometimes the speed of your WiFi can be affected by your neighbors. If your neighbor has a slower WiFi than you do, and if your routers happen to be using the same frequency channel, your WiFi speed will drop to the speed of your neighbor's router. There are 14 frequency channels, so this is rare, but it can happen.

For these reasons, I often tell players that it might be better to play on a mobile network. Mobile networks, like 3G, 4G, 5G, and LTE, are more reliable than WiFi, especially if you live in a crowded area.

What does my ⭐️ mean?

Stars represent an ELO rating system. The crucial feature of Elo rating system is this: beating a higher-rated opponent gives you more ⭐️ than beating one with a lower rating. Similarly, when you lose to a player with a lower Elo score, your rating will decrease more than if the opponent had a higher initial rating.

To make progress you must be better than yourself, better then expected. Once you reach Your skill level, you probably will stay there.

ELO rating calculation is described here

You can receive a more ⭐️ in big L table, while XS table gives you the lowest amount of ⭐️.

In "One vs all" game the ELO ranking will be calculated as You play with each play separately but with lower factor to avoid big jumps with ⭐️.

I have a new mobile phone. How can I transfer my Yamb account and my scores to new device?

1. Open Yamb on the old device go to your profile and sign in with your Google account.

2. Do the same on the new device and you will be signed in on new and signed out from the old device. That's it.

I have lost my account and scores, can I get it back?

If you didn't sign in before you can't get it back yourself. Contact us to yamb.igre@gmail.com and tell us what was your old alias or nickname.

What does my ranking mean?

Ranking is your position on leaderboard with 💎.

What does my ⭐️ mean?

Stars represent an ELO ranking system. Every player starts with 1000⭐️ and gets more stars if he defeats a stronger opponent.

What does 🤝 mean?

It is used as fair play measure. For example 5/7 🤝 means that player has played totally 7 games against other player and he interupt the game 2 times.

What does 🏆 mean?

It says how many times you won in the skill league.

What does 💣 mean?

It says how many times you won the fallout competition.

What does 🥨 mean?

It says how many times you won in multiplayer game. It is actaully measure for player experience.
